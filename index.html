    mixs-missing-value-sandbox

    Name: mixs-missing-value-sandbox

    Classes

    Class Description
    Checklist A collection of metadata terms (slots) to minimally describe the sampling and sequencing method of a specimen used to generate a nucleotide sequence.
            Mims Metagenome or Environmental
    Extension A collection of recommended metadata terms (slots) developed by community experts, describing the specific context under which a sample was collected.
            Soil A collection of terms appropriate when collecting samples and sequencing samples obtained from the uppermost layer of Earth's crust, contributed by the Terragenome Consortium.
                    MimsSoil MIxS Data that comply with the Mims checklist and the Soil Extension
    MixsCompliantData A collection of Data that comply with some combination of a MIxS checklist and environmental extension
